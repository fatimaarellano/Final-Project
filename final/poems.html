1.

Pick up your head
look me in the eyes
And see the world
But...don't look so sad

The longing in your eyes
The pain that once residedin your past
The beautiful smile lost in the mirror
Fat, ugly, skinny, the voices in my head
they have names, Sue..
Ana, and Deb
Am I normal?

Out of place
No happy memories
There are chains hagign over my voice
Swords coming at me form every direction
Mental illness don't exist
It is just in your head just think positive
Put on a facade

I'm getting better I'm happy
There is no pain
I love myself
A beautiful lie covers me in a.....
Pile of mask and a never ending world
You want me to be okay
I am...I guess

The happy girl yu once knew is nowhere to be found
Am I pretty yet?
Am I good enough yet?
Am I there yet?
I'm slowly breaking down...
I don't know who I am anymore
I feel like a canary caged in
The door is opened but...

i have no motivation to get out.
It's as if my feet are cemented onto the cage
Every door to freedom can be opened
they can be so close but
I won't be able to get out.

I tell myself that
I'm happy
it shows but only for a few moments
I'm constantly tyring to be conformed with life
Trying to not harm those around
Or...myself.
But I guess in the end
I Am Okay

By Jacqueline Ortega

2.

You say you love me
Yet you treat me like I don’t exist
For that I don’t know why….
I can’t get you out of my head.

For some odd reason
My head says no
My heart says yes
The people around me see you as a jerk
But I don’t seem to understand

It’s as if you made me fall for you and then put blindfolds on me
So that I could back you up till it fell off.

Its as if I love you were a lie
Did you lie to me when you hugged me and said, “I’ll be there for you”?
Or “they have to get through me first.”

Maybe it was just child’s play
Maybe it was all just a dream
Maybe it wasn’t a lie but an experiment
Maybe if it hadn’t happened this feeling would’ve never been here.

By Jacqueline Ortega

3.

I haven’t been feeling much lately
I’m not numb
I just.. I don’t know
A lot of people tell me I look happy
I should keep it up
It’s nice to see me smile

I wish I could show the world my genuine smile
At the moment it is nowhere to be found
Living life because I have to not because I want to
I am in a time machine that is stuck in the past
The present has been erased
The future is gone, seemingly impossible

“You have grown, we see so much progress”
I suppose I will lock up the pandora box again,
Suppress it, avoid it
Never looking back, because if I do the “progress” will be gone

Watch the sky
It’s beautiful how the clouds move in one direction
As if they always know where they are going
All in different shapes
Shapeshifting into forms unknown to them
I wish I could shapeshift out of my own skin
Mold it and perfect it
Not to the point where everything seems like plastic
But I want to make it safe again

My body and soul reject each other
How am I supposed to pretend I am fine?
There is war rumbling inside of me
One can only see the chaos shown
Not the reality under the lock.
I have urges to participate in things that..
I would usually object to
I have a need to get high or drunk
Stop don’t listen
My soul is trying to pull me away
Be you, the one I know
I don’t know if she still exists
I’m sorry
I’m so sorry
I wonder if I can ever repair myself
Be the one people love
So far all that can mustered up is self hate

I’m confident or that’s how it seems
I’m a badass, that’s my appearance talking
I look kept together and can totally make your day
I’m the weird hyper girl
The one that is always smiling
The one who is gullible and easily flustered
Everyone believes my cover
I don’t know who I truly am
I’m a work in progress stuck in a broken time machine.

By Jacqueline ortega
